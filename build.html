Logistics &
Execution.

Executing multi-million Rand infrastructure in remote locations requires more than blueprints. It requires an integrated supply chain, owned heavy machinery, and an artisan workforce ready to deploy.

Internal Plant Fleet

By owning our own fleet of heavy earthmoving equipment and logistics vehicles, we eliminate reliance on third-party rentals and drastically reduce downtime.

Directly Employed Artisans

Quality control is maintained because the men and women tying our rebar and pouring our concrete are directly employed, heavily trained Unicon personnel.

Remote Mobilization

Our operational nodes are equipped to set up fully self-sufficient construction camps in hostile, off-grid environments anywhere in the region.

Strict Safety Protocols

Our safety files and active site management comply with the highest international standards, resulting in our flawless Zero-Harm track record.
