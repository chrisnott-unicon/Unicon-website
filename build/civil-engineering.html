Phase 2: Build / Transport & Civils

Transport &
Earthworks.

Unicon executes the heavy civil infrastructure that connects the continent. We engineer and build multi-span river bridges, severe-duty haulage roads, and massive topographical platforms.

Heavy Execution

The Foundation
of Progress

Before vertical structures can rise, the landscape must be tamed. Unicon deploys its internal fleet of heavy earthmoving plant to execute massive cut-to-fill operations, stabilized by retaining walls and drainage networks.

Bridge Construction

Expertise in both pre-cast beam lifting and continuous in-situ concrete deck pouring for highway overpasses, river crossings, and heavy agricultural transport bridges. We manage deep piling and abutment reinforcement.

Cut-to-Fill Earthworks

Execution of massive volumetric earthmoving operations. We reshape hostile topographies, balancing the site to minimize material import and export, creating perfectly graded platform terraces for commercial development.

Severe-Duty Roads

Construction of municipal access roads, agricultural haulage tracks, and industrial paving layers. Designed and compacted to withstand the continuous punishment of 40-ton articulated truck traffic without subsidence.

Retaining Structures

Engineering and constructing heavy gabion baskets, reinforced concrete retaining walls, and soil-nailed embankments to secure massive landforms and prevent slippage in high-rainfall regions.

Tech Integration

Drone Surveying & Volumetrics

Earthworks relies entirely on accurate data. Unicon deploys advanced drone photogrammetry to map out sites before we break ground. This provides our site agents with hyper-accurate 3D topographical meshes, allowing us to calculate cut-to-fill volumes down to the cubic meter, preventing massive cost overruns during the excavation phase.
