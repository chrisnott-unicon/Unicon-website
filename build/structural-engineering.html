Concrete Domes & Curves

Erecting massive, unsupported concrete dome roofs requires absolute mastery of physics and formwork. Unicon executes perfectly smooth, curved shuttering and post-tensioned walls to create highly resilient, column-free containment structures.

High Vertical Frameworks

From multi-story retail basements to towering university campuses, we construct the rigid skeletons that support modern infrastructure. Our execution spans heavy load-bearing columns, suspended slabs, and structural steel integration.

High-Altitude Scaffolding

Pouring structural concrete hundreds of feet in the air demands extreme safety protocols and complex temporary support structures. We engineer and deploy massive scaffolding networks to hold multi-ton loads during the curing phase.

High-Density Reinforcement

The backbone of our structures. We meticulously interpret engineered rebar schedules, tying high-density steel meshes that grant our concrete extreme tensile strength and flexibility against seismic and load-bearing pressures.
