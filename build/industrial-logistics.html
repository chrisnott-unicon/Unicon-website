High Load Structural Execution

Logistics &
Freight Civils

The explosion of African e-commerce and international freight demands immense structural durability. We execute the heavy civils that allow logistics properties to handle millions of tons of high-velocity cargo without structural fatigue.

Distribution Centres

Turnkey construction of mega-warehouses. We specialize in pouring laser-screeded, "super flat" jointless concrete floors required for high-bay, automated forklift operations.

Port & Freight Hardstands

Execution of severe-duty external concrete and block-paved yards. Engineered specifically to withstand the extreme point-loads of reach stackers and fully loaded shipping containers.

Cold Chain Facilities

Building highly insulated structures for the agricultural and pharmaceutical cold-chain. We integrate complex MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) routing for massive industrial refrigeration plants.
