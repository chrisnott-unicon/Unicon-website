Civil Balance of Plant (BOP)

Power Gen
Foundations

Before a single panel or blade is installed, the civil infrastructure must be flawless. From massive, high-density rebar pours for turbine gravity bases to immense site-clearing operations, Unicon delivers the ground-up certainty required by EPCs and IPPs.

Wind Turbine Civils

High-volume, continuous concrete pours for gravity and piled turbine foundations. We master the incredibly dense steel reinforcement matrices and anchor cage leveling required for modern megawatt turbines.

Solar PV Earthworks

Extensive civil preparation for utility-scale solar arrays. Services include Lidar-mapped cut-and-fill terracing, trenching for extensive cable networks, and the construction of inverter/transformer plinths.

BESS & Substation Civils

Construction of Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) container platforms, heavily reinforced substation structural frames, and severe-duty internal site roads for abnormal load component deliveries.
