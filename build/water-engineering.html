Zero-Leak Tolerances

Concrete
Mastery

Liquid retaining structures leave no room for error. A single millimeter of variance can compromise millions of liters of municipal water. Unicon deploys specialized shuttering, post-tensioned cabling, and advanced waterproofing to guarantee absolute containment.

Post-Tensioned Reservoirs

Construction of massive municipal and agricultural reservoirs (up to 40 Megalitres). We utilize hydraulically tensioned steel cables sleeved within the concrete walls to actively counteract outward hydrostatic pressure, ensuring crack-free walls.

Wastewater Plants (WWTW)

Executing the heavy concrete civil works for large-scale clariflocculators, deep filtration beds, and oxidation tanks critical to regional sanitation grids. Perfect shutter finishes extend asset lifespans against chemical erosion.

Bulk Pipeline Networks

Deep trenching, bedding, and laying of large-diameter steel, HDPE, and concrete pipelines across hostile African topographies to connect isolated communities and industrial hubs to the primary water grid.
