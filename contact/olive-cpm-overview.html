The African Context

Remote Execution.
Total Control.

Executing major infrastructure across Southern Africa comes with inherent risks. Geographical isolation, fragmented supply chains, extreme weather events, and delayed reporting plague traditional contracting.

For developers and stakeholders sitting in boardrooms in Johannesburg, London, or Dubai, managing a project in a deep-rural node often feels like looking into a black box. Olive CPM was built by Unicon to shatter that black box.

Contractual Defence

By enforcing rigid, timestamped daily site records, Olive actively builds a defensive wall of data against unfair penalties and secures valid Extensions of Time (EOT).

Live Telemetry

No more waiting for month-end reports. Stakeholders gain immediate, cloud-based access to site weather data, labour counts, material deliveries, and geo-tagged progress photos.
