Chronology

The Evolution of
Unicon

1991

Foundation & Early Roots

Founded by Tony Nott, Unicon Construction began its journey with a strong community focus, specializing in the construction of rural government clinics, critical healthcare facilities, and educational campuses across KwaZulu-Natal.

Commercial Expansion (2000s)

A rapid diversification phase. Unicon successfully transitioned into the private sector, executing massive multi-story retail malls, specialized factory fit-outs, and heavy concrete machine bases for blue-chip clients like Nestle.

2010

The Turnkey Pivot

Recognizing a critical market shift, we invested heavily in an in-house design office. This allowed us to pioneer Design and Build methods, providing clients with holistic, risk-mitigated construction solutions.

Mastery of Civil Infrastructure

Our expertise evolved into heavy municipal civils. We established specialized systems—dominating the sector in water retaining structures, massive post-tensioned 40ML+ reservoirs, and provincial bridges.
