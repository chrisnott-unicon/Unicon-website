Bridging Civil Mastery with Biological Harmony.

Unicon Construction formally details its strategic alliance with JWE Africa, executing turnkey Agricultural EPC solutions across the continent.

Heavy civil execution requires flawless biological and operational design. A mega-dairy or intensive feedlot is effectively an organic factory; if the physical infrastructure does not support the daily workflow and biological needs of the livestock, the asset fails to generate a return on investment.

To eliminate this friction, Unicon Construction has formed a powerful strategic alliance with JWE Africa Agri and Industrial Solutions. This partnership creates a true Engineer, Procure, and Construct (EPC) powerhouse tailored specifically for the African agricultural market.

African Reality Engineering

The JWE team, spearheaded by Founder and Project Director Emmanuel Houart, brings over 30 years of specialized experience across Africa and the Middle East. They specialize in what we term "African Reality Engineering"—translating complex agricultural requirements into robust, highly efficient physical infrastructure.

  • Thermodynamic Barn Design: Creating altitude-adjusted ventilation and heat-stress mitigation structures.
  • Biosecurity & Herd Flow: Designing shifting Lines of Separation (LOS) for phased farm expansions and disease control.
  • Veterinary Infrastructure: Led by Dr. Tanya Hughes, integrating world-class clinical standards into on-site hospital pens and maternity wards.

Operations Informing Infrastructure

A structural shell is useless without operational context. That is why the JWE partnership bridges the critical gap between heavy civil design and practical farm management.

With expertise driven by Platinum Master Dairyman Bruce Braithwaite—the only person to win the Royal Agricultural Society Gold Cup six times—the alliance ensures that Unicon’s physical builds (from rotary milking parlours to Total Mixed Ration (TMR) feed complexes) are perfectly calibrated to maximize herd yield, automate processes, and simplify staff workflows.

By uniting Unicon's 35 years of mass earthworks and concrete mastery with JWE's biological intelligence, we deliver absolute project certainty for the region's most demanding agricultural developers.
