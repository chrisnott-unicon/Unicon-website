A Legacy of Impact

Milestones measured not just in concrete poured, but in lives changed and communities empowered.

1991

Humble Beginnings & Community Clinics

Founded by Tony Nott, our first projects were deeply rooted in rural KwaZulu-Natal. By building desperately needed government clinics and schools, we established our foundational commitment to community upliftment and local job creation.

2010

The Turnkey Pivot: Empowering Local Engineers

Recognizing the need for better project execution, we brought architectural and engineering design in-house. This allowed us to hire and upskill local engineering talent, fostering a tight-knit, collaborative culture that bridges the gap between the drawing board and the construction site.

Securing Water for Millions

As our capacity grew, we shifted focus to massive liquid-retaining structures. Delivering 40ML+ reservoirs and critical wastewater plants didn't just showcase our technical prowess—it secured clean, safe water infrastructure for millions of South Africans.
