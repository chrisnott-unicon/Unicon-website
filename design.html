Our Methodology

Single-Point
Accountability.

Traditional design-bid-build models naturally create adversarial relationships between consultants and contractors, leading to timeline delays, scope gaps, and budget overruns.

Unicon consolidates the architectural, engineering, and estimation processes in-house. By integrating our site agents during the drafting phase, every blueprint is rigorously tested for real-world constructability before ground is even broken. This is the definition of true Turnkey EPC.

Contract Formats Supported

Specialists in JBCC, GCC & FIDIC Turnkey

Early Cost Certainty

We run design and estimation concurrently, providing highly accurate Bills of Quantities (BOQs) upfront. This transparent process allows developers and municipal clients to secure funding and make rapid financial decisions without fear of hidden variations.

Engineering Coordination

Our multidisciplinary approach integrates structural engineers, architects, and site agents from day one. This holistic synergy drastically reduces design fees and eliminates the technical scope gaps that typically plague large-scale infrastructure builds.

4D BIM Integration

Utilizing advanced 3D Building Information Modeling (BIM) to ensure absolute clash detection. We map out rebar schedules, MEP routing, and spatial planning digitally, ensuring a seamless transition into the physical execution phase.

Speed to Market

By bypassing the protracted, multi-stage traditional tender period, we are able to mobilize our heavy plant, secure cross-border logistics, and break ground months ahead of standard contracting models.
