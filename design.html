Our Methodology

Solution-Based
Engineering

Traditional design-bid-build models create friction, delays, and cost overruns. Unicon’s in-house design capacity eliminates these barriers, providing clients with high professional quality designs that are optimized for immediate execution.

Early Costings & Rapid Response

We prioritize quicker client responses. By running design and estimation concurrently, we provide highly accurate early costings. This allows clients to make rapid financial decisions and secure funding without waiting months for traditional tender documentation.

Integrated Constructability

A design is only as good as its execution. Our structural engineers work directly alongside our site agents during the drafting phase. Every blueprint is rigorously tested for real-world constructability, eliminating clashes and site delays before ground is even broken.

High Professional Quality

Speed does not compromise our standards. Our in-house design office utilizes industry-leading BIM software to produce high-resolution structural detailing and architectural planning that meets stringent ISO, GCC, and FIDIC professional standards.

Urgent MEA Roll-Out

When disaster strikes or critical infrastructure requires urgent deployment across the Middle East and Africa (MEA), our integrated design capacity allows us to mobilize cross-border operations months ahead of traditional contractors.
