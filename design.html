Our Design Philosophy

Single-Point
Accountability.

Traditional design-bid-build models naturally create adversarial relationships between consultants and contractors, leading to timeline delays and budget overruns.

Unicon consolidates the architectural, engineering, and estimation processes in-house. By integrating our site agents during the drafting phase, every blueprint is rigorously tested for real-world constructability before ground is even broken.

Early Cost Certainty

We run design and estimation concurrently, providing highly accurate Bills of Quantities (BOQs) upfront.

Engineering Coordination

Our multidciplinary approach integrates structural engineers, architects, and site agents from day one.
